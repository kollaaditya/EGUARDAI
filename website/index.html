Hackathon MVP

Spot early dropout risk with a transparent, student-first AI.

EduGuard AI uses attendance, internal marks, and assignment delays to predict risk levels (Low/Medium/High) so educators can intervene early.

3 Inputs

Simple, explainable signals.

1 Minute

Setup for a working pilot.

Real-time

Instant risk predictions.

Why it matters

Early alerts help mentors act fast—before absenteeism or missed assignments become permanent dropouts.

  • Transparent model with clear feature importance.
  • Designed for hackathons and quick demos.
  • Built on Python, pandas, and scikit-learn.

Project Overview

EduGuard AI is a lightweight MVP that predicts dropout risk using a Random Forest Classifier. It is designed for clarity—every prediction includes a simple explanation of what drove the result.

Input Signals

Attendance %, internal marks, and assignment delay days.

Output

Risk levels: Low, Medium, or High dropout probability.

Intervention

Actionable alerts for counselors and faculty.
